How does the AI Agent work?

Classification Process:

  1. Topic Detection: Identifies relevant categories (Connector, API/SDK, SSO, etc.)
  2. Sentiment Analysis: Determines customer emotional state
  3. Priority Assessment: Evaluates urgency based on business impact

Response Strategy:

  • RAG Topics: How-to, Product, Best practices, API/SDK, SSO ‚Üí AI generates detailed answers
  • Routing Topics: Connector, Lineage, Glossary, Sensitive data ‚Üí Route to specialists

Confidence Scoring:

  • High confidence (>0.8): Fully automated response
  • Medium confidence (0.6-0.8): AI response with human review
  • Low confidence (<0.6): Route to human agent
